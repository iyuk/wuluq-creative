We Help You With

Our machine learning engine creates lookalike models from your best-performing customer segments. By layering custom algorithms on industry-leading data sets, we identify and reach customers you won’t find in other channels.

Programmatic triggers allow you to automatically mail prospects that engage with your website. Using identity resolution, website visits are matched to home addresses so you can reach both anonymous and logged-in visitors.

Postie algorithms identify the audience segments hidden in your 1st party CRM data. With precise segmentation, you can deploy highly personalized campaigns and convert customers that aren’t responding to other marketing efforts.
